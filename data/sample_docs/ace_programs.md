# Adult and Continuing Education (A.C.E) Programs - Kwekwe Polytechnic

## Program Overview
The Adult and Continuing Education (A.C.E) Division provides practical skills training and professional development opportunities for adult learners, focusing on career enhancement and skills development.

## Entry Requirements Summary
- **Minimum Age:** 16 years for most programs
- **Educational Requirements:** 5 O' Level subjects including English Language
- **Additional Requirements:** May include aptitude tests, interviews, or portfolio reviews
- **Special Requirements:** Some programs may have specific health or physical requirements

## Program Duration
- **National Certificate Programs:** 1-2 years
- **Short Courses:** 3 months to 1 year
- **Apprenticeship Programs:** 2-4 years
- **Outreach Programs:** Varies by program
