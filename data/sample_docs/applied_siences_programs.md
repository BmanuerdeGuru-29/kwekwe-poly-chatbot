## Entry Requirements

### Standard Requirements for All Applied Sciences Programs
- Minimum 5 Ordinary Level Passes including:
  - Mathematics (Grade C or better)
  - English Language (Grade C or better)
  - A relevant Science subject (Grade C or better)
  - Any other 2 subjects (Grade C or better)

### Specific Science Requirements
- **Information Technology:** Physical Science or Computer Science preferred
- **Food Science:** Biology, Chemistry, or Agricultural Science preferred
- **Metallurgical Programs:** Chemistry, Physics, or Physical Science preferred
- **Laboratory Technology:** Biology, Chemistry, or Physics preferred
