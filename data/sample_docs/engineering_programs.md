## Entry Requirements

### General Requirements for All Engineering Programs
- Minimum 5 Ordinary Level Passes including:
  - Mathematics (Grade C or better)
  - English Language (Grade C or better)
  - A relevant Science Subject (Grade C or better)
  - Any other 2 subjects (Grade C or better)

### National Diploma (ND) Additional Requirements
- Applicants must possess the relevant National Certificate (NC) in the same field

## Program Duration
- **National Certificate (NC):** 1 year (typically)
- **National Diploma (ND):** 2-3 years (depending on program)
- **Full-time study mode**
