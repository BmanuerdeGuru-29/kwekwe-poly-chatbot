# Kwekwe Polytechnic - Institutional Overview

Kwekwe Polytechnic is a leading technical and vocational education institution in Zimbabwe, dedicated to providing quality technical education and skills development.

## Academic Divisions
Kwekwe Polytechnic offers education through five main divisions:
1. Engineering Division
2. Commerce Division  
3. Applied Sciences Division
4. Bachelor of Technology (B-Tech) Programs
5. Adult and Continuing Education (A.C.E) Programs

## Accreditation
All programs are HEXCO (Higher Education Examination Council) certified, ensuring national recognition and quality standards.
