# HEXCO Results and Examinations - Kwekwe Polytechnic

## HEXCO Overview

### What is HEXCO?
HEXCO (Higher Education Examination Council) is the national body responsible for technical and vocational education examinations in Zimbabwe. HEXCO ensures:

- Standardization of technical education qualifications
- Quality assurance in technical training
- National recognition of technical qualifications
- Industry-relevant assessment standards

### HEXCO Qualifications Offered
- **National Certificate (NC):** Foundation technical qualification
- **National Diploma (ND):** Advanced technical qualification
- **Higher National Diploma (HND):** Specialized advanced qualification
- **Bachelor of Technology (B-Tech):** Degree-level technical qualification

### Examination Types

#### Theory Examinations
- **Format:** Written examinations
- **Duration:** 2-4 hours per subject
- **Coverage:** Theoretical knowledge and understanding
- **Weighting:** Typically 60-70% of final grade

#### Practical Examinations
- **Format:** Hands-on assessment
- **Duration:** Varies by program (2-8 hours)
- **Coverage:** Practical skills and competencies
- **Weighting:** Typically 30-40% of final grade

#### Continuous Assessment
- **Components:** Assignments, projects, class tests
- **Weighting:** 20-30% of final grade
- **Purpose:** Ongoing evaluation of student progress

### Result Categories

#### Pass Grades
- **Distinction:** 75% and above
- **Credit:** 65% - 74%
- **Pass:** 50% - 64%
- **Class Award:** Based on overall performance

#### Fail Grades
- **Fail:** Below 50%
- **Supplementary:** 45% - 49% (eligible for supplementary exams)
- **Repeat:** Below 45% (must repeat the subject)

## Certification Process

### Certificate Types

#### National Certificate (NC)
- **Awarded:** Upon successful completion of NC program
- **Requirements:** Pass all subjects with minimum 50%
- **Processing Time:** 3-6 months after results release

#### National Diploma (ND)
- **Awarded:** Upon successful completion of ND program
- **Requirements:** NC qualification + ND subjects passed
- **Processing Time:** 3-6 months after results release

#### Higher National Diploma (HND)
- **Awarded:** Upon successful completion of HND program
- **Requirements:** ND qualification + HND subjects passed
- **Processing Time:** 6-12 months after results release

#### Bachelor of Technology (B-Tech)
- **Awarded:** Upon successful completion of degree program
- **Requirements:** HND qualification + B-Tech requirements
- **Processing Time:** 6-12 months after graduation

## Examination Policies

### Registration Requirements
- **Academic Requirements:** Minimum attendance (80%)
- **Fee Payment:** Full examination fees paid
- **Registration Form:** Completed and submitted
- **Identity Documents:** Valid identification

### Examination Rules
- **Punctuality:** Arrive 30 minutes before examination
- **Identification:** Student ID required
- **Materials:** Only allowed materials permitted
- **Conduct:** Strict examination conduct enforced

### Special Arrangements

#### Special Needs Students
- **Extra Time:** Additional time for documented conditions
- **Special Equipment:** Adaptive technology where needed
- **Separate Venue:** Quiet environment when required
- **Documentation:** Medical certificate required

#### Deferred Examinations
- **Valid Reasons:** Medical emergencies, bereavement
- **Application Process:** Formal application with evidence
- **Approval Process:** Review by examinations committee
- **Timing:** Usually scheduled with supplementary exams

## Appeals and Re-marking

### Appeal Process
- **Application Period:** Within 2 weeks of results release
- **Application Form:** Available from examinations office
- **Appeal Fee:** Non-refundable fee applies
- **Processing Time:** 4-6 weeks

### Re-marking Procedure
- **Subject to Appeal:** Specific subjects only
- **Independent Markers:** Different examiners re-mark scripts
- **Outcome:** May result in higher, lower, or same grade
- **Final Decision:** Examination committee decision is binding
