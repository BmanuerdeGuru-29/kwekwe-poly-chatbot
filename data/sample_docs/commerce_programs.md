# Commerce Division - Kwekwe Polytechnic

## Division Structure
The Commerce Division offers programs through two main departments focused on business and management education.

## Departments and Heads

### Management Department
- **Head:** Mr. T. Sambama
- **Programs Offered:**
  - Office Management (National Certificate, National Diploma)
  - Human Resources Management (National Certificate, National Diploma)
  - Sales & Marketing Management (National Certificate, National Diploma)
  - Purchasing & Supply Management (National Certificate, National Diploma)
  - Records Management & Information Science (National Certificate, National Diploma)

### Business Studies Department
- **Head:** Mr. A. Vuma
- **Programs Offered:**
  - Accountancy (National Certificate, National Diploma)
  - Banking and Finance (National Certificate, National Diploma, Higher National Diploma)

## Entry Requirements

### Management Department Programs

#### National Certificate (NC) Entry Requirements:
- Minimum 5 Ordinary Level Passes
- English Language must be at grade C or better
- Any other 4 subjects at grade C or better

#### National Diploma (ND) Entry Requirements:
- Applicants must possess the relevant National Certificate (NC) in Management or related field

### Business Studies Department Programs

#### National Certificate (NC) Entry Requirements:
- Minimum 5 Ordinary Level Passes
- Mathematics at grade C or better
- English Language at grade C or better
- Any other 3 subjects at grade C or better

#### National Diploma (ND) Entry Requirements:
- Applicants must possess the relevant National Certificate (NC) in Business Studies

#### Higher National Diploma (HND) Entry Requirements:
- Applicants must possess the relevant National Diploma (ND) in Banking and Finance

## Program Duration
- **National Certificate (NC):** 1 year full-time
- **National Diploma (ND):** 2 years full-time
- **Higher National Diploma (HND):** 1-2 years full-time

## Accreditation
- All programs are HEXCO certified
- Nationally recognized qualifications
- Industry-aligned curriculum
- Professional body recognition where applicable

## Program Details

### Office Management
Focuses on administrative skills, office procedures, and organizational management. Graduates can work as office administrators, executive assistants, and office managers.

### Human Resources Management
Covers recruitment, training, employee relations, and strategic HR management. Career paths include HR officers, training coordinators, and HR managers.

### Sales & Marketing Management
Emphasizes marketing strategies, sales techniques, and customer relationship management. Graduates work in sales, marketing departments, and customer service.

### Purchasing & Supply Management
Focuses on procurement, supply chain management, and inventory control. Career opportunities include procurement officers and supply chain managers.

### Records Management & Information Science
Covers information management, archival systems, and documentation. Graduates work in records departments and information centers.

### Accountancy
Provides comprehensive accounting education including financial accounting, cost accounting, and taxation. Graduates can pursue careers as accountants, auditors, and financial officers.

### Banking and Finance
Focuses on banking operations, financial services, and investment management. Career paths include bank officers, financial advisors, and credit analysts.
