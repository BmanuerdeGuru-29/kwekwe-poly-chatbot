## Admissions Process

### January 2026 Intake

#### Application Status
- **Status:** Re-advertised
- **Applications:** Currently being accepted
- **Opening Dates:** Announced for January 2026 Intake Students

#### Application Methods
1. **Online Application:**
   - Portal: https://apply.kwekwepoly.ac.zw
   - Available 24/7
   - Immediate confirmation

2. **Manual Application:**
   - Collect forms from admissions office
   - Submit completed forms with required documents
   - Processing time: 2-3 weeks

#### Required Documents
- **Academic Transcripts:** Original and certified copies
- **Birth Certificate:** Certified copy
- **National ID:** Certified copy
- **Passport-size Photos:** 2 recent photos
- **Application Fee:** USD $20 (non-refundable)

### General Admission Requirements

#### Minimum Academic Requirements
- **5 Ordinary Level Passes** including:
  - English Language (Grade C or better)
  - Mathematics (Grade C or better) for most programs
  - Relevant Science subjects for technical programs
  - Any other subjects as specified by program

#### Program-Specific Requirements
- **Engineering Programs:** Mathematics, English, and Science at Grade C or better
- **Commerce Programs:** English at Grade C, Mathematics for some programs
- **Applied Sciences:** Mathematics, English, and relevant Science at Grade C
- **B-Tech Programs:** Relevant technical certificates and Journeyman class 1
- **A.C.E Programs:** 5 O' Levels including English

#### Age Requirements
- **Minimum Age:** 16 years for most programs
- **Maximum Age:** No upper age limit for A.C.E programs
- **Traditional Programs:** Typically under 25 years

### Special Admissions

#### Mature Entry
- **Age:** 25 years and above
- **Experience:** Minimum 5 years relevant work experience
- **Requirements:** May include entrance examinations and interviews

#### Foreign Students
- **Requirements:** Valid study permit
- **Qualifications:** Equivalent qualifications verification
- **Language:** English proficiency proof (if required)
- **Fees:** Foreign student rates may apply

#### Students with Disabilities
- **Facilities:** Accessible infrastructure available
- **Support:** Special examination arrangements
- **Documentation:** Medical certificate required

### Selection Process

#### Selection Criteria
1. **Academic Merit:** Based on O' Level results
2. **Program Requirements:** Specific subject grades
3. **Interview Performance:** For selected programs
4. **Aptitude Tests:** For technical programs
5. **Portfolio Review:** For creative programs

#### Interview Process
- **Purpose:** Assess suitability and motivation
- **Format:** Face-to-face or online
- **Duration:** 15-30 minutes
- **Outcome:** Communicated within 2 weeks

### Registration Process

#### Successful Applicants
1. **Offer Letter:** Receive admission offer
2. **Acceptance:** Accept offer within specified period
3. **Medical Examination:** Complete medical check-up
4. **Fee Payment:** Pay required fees
5. **Registration:** Complete registration formalities
6. **Orientation:** Attend orientation program

#### Registration Requirements
- **Original Documents:** For verification
- **Medical Certificate:** From approved medical practitioner
- **Parental Consent:** For applicants under 18 years
- **Proof of Payment:** Fee payment receipts
