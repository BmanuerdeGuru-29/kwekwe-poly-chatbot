# Bachelor of Technology (B-Tech) Programs - Kwekwe Polytechnic

## Program Overview
Kwekwe Polytechnic offers Bachelor of Technology (B-Tech) Honors degree programs designed to provide advanced technical education and specialized engineering pathways for students seeking higher-level qualifications.

## Available B-Tech Programs

### B-Tech (Honors) Degree in Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering
- **Coordinator:** Mr. B. Kufa
- **Program Level:** Bachelor's Degree (Honors)
- **Duration:** 4-5 years (depending on entry qualifications)

#### Entry Requirements:
1. **Academic Requirements:**
   - 5 O level subjects including:
     - English Language (Grade C or better)
     - Mathematics (Grade C or better)
     - A relevant Science subject (Grade C or better)
     - Any other 2 subjects (Grade C or better)

2. **Technical Qualifications:**
   - National Certificate in one of the following:
     - Machineshop Engineering
     - Fabrication Engineering
     - Refrigeration and Air Conditioning
     - Millwrights
     - Foundry
     - Draughting and Design
     - Or equivalent qualification

3. **Professional Certification:**
   - Journeyman class 1 certificate

#### Program Focus:
- Advanced manufacturing processes
- Industrial engineering principles
- Manufacturing systems design
- Quality engineering
- Production management
- Industrial automation
- Project management

### B-Tech (Honors) Degree in Electrical Power Engineering
- **Coordinator:** Mr. J. Makonese
- **Program Level:** Bachelor's Degree (Honors)
- **Duration:** 4-5 years (depending on entry qualifications)

#### Entry Requirements:
1. **Academic Requirements:**
   - 5 O level subjects including:
     - English Language (Grade C or better)
     - Mathematics (Grade C or better)
     - A relevant Science subject (Grade C or better)
     - Any other 2 subjects (Grade C or better)

2. **Technical Qualifications (Progressive):**
   - National Certificate in Electrical Power Engineering
   - Journeyman class 1 certificate
   - National Diploma (ND) in Electrical Power Engineering or equivalent
   - Higher National Diploma (HND) in:
     - Electrical Power Engineering OR
     - Instrumentation and Control Engineering

#### Program Focus:
- Advanced electrical power systems
- Power generation and distribution
- Electrical machine design
- Power electronics
- Control systems
- Renewable energy systems
- Power system analysis and protection
